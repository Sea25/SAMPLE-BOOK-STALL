about our stall

our story

paper & oak began as a small dream on college street — a place where stories find their readers. we believe in the magic of physical books, the smell of old pages, and the joy of discovering your next favourite read.

our space

tucked away in the bustling college street, our stall is a quiet corner for book lovers. we handpick every book that finds a place on our shelves, from literary fiction to speculative worlds.
